Q & A

  1. How to Upload Music?


          Click the Share Music button on the bar. Then you will see there is a form for you to enter the information of the music.

  2. What is the price?


          In this website, user can set the price of the music if they want. Other user can pay for your music upload to listen your music. Our website will charge you 10% of the service charge if the music need user to pay.

  3. How can I change the information of the account?


          To change the information, click on the button "Change information" at the right-bottom of the acccount information and you can change it.

  4. How can I check the music uploaded?


          Click the Music Uploaded button. There will be a list of music shown with the date that you uploaded music.
